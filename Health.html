Date: 10/5/22
Health and Wellness while Gaming
Article link: https://www.frontiersin.org/articles/10.3389/fspor.2021.693700/full Links to an external site.
1. The article discusses the health risks the majority of gamers and esports players face, as well as how keeping healthy habits and a normal exercise routine could lessen these risks. Because they experience high amounts of physical activity and sedentary behavior, esports players are at high risk of suffering from non-communicable diseases and early death. The article also promotes using digital technology for promoting healthy habits; sleep and heart rate trackers can bring awareness to the health of many gamers. Focusing on getting better sleep, a good diet, and regular exercise can contribute to improving the health and mental well being of a multitude of gamers.
2. My most healthiest habit is drinking water. I *occasionally* lift weights and work out, but I skip leg day every day. Other than that, I have a terrible sleep schedule and diet; I often skip meals because of a fast-paced schedule.
3. I would say incorporating physical activity into my gaming sessions would be helpful! I have a VR headset at home and played BeatSaber prior to this school year; I think playing games that require movement will help improve my health.
4. It is important to know about these things when thinking about joining the esports field because, just like any job, it is important to prioritize one's own health. Lacking in physical, mental, or emotional well-being can lead to fatigue, depression, or even worse. Being on top of your health can help you perform in games better!

Date: 10/7/22 PC Gaming Health
1. Respond: After gaming for a long period of time, what feels tired? Eyes, hands, mind, wrists, neck, etc. What can you do differently to alleviate pain or tiredness when gaming?
After gaming for a long period time, my eyes feel tired, my shoulders are in pain, and my wrists feel tired. After the adrenaline from gaming wears out, my mind feels exhausted and I am unable to do anything productive afterwards. I think taking breaks, having a proper posture, and doing stretches between matches for games would help alleviate some pain and tiredness when gaming.
2. Read this article: Exercises for Wrist Pain
The article details a variety of wrist stretches to help relieve wrist pain. These include wrist circles, wrist rotations, wrist flexor stretches, and prayer stretches. These stretches help exercise your wrist and improve their flexibility.
From this article, I could follow the instructions on several stretches and incorporate them into my gaming sessions! Whether I am dying in league of legends, or I need to take a break, I can do some wrist stretches to relieve some stress and pain!
Article link: https://www.eliteorthopaedic.com/blog/wrist-pain-exercises
